What is the E-Learning Platform?

User Accounts

Secure registration and login with form validation and local storage session management.

📬

Direct Contact

Integrated EmailJS contact system that sends real email notifications to the team.

📱

Fully Responsive

Optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices for a seamless experience everywhere.

👨‍🏫

Meet Instructors

Dedicated section to showcase tutors, their expertise, and course offerings.

⚛️

React Powered

02 — Goals

Project Objectives

Key goals driving the platform's development

1
Provide a modern, accessible online learning platform for students
2
Allow users to register and log in with secure credential management
3
Display available courses in a clear and organized layout
4
Introduce tutors and instructors to help students make informed choices
5
Enable communication through an integrated Contact Us form
6
Deliver a fully responsive design across all device sizes
